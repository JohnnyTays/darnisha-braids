Within a 15-mile radius: 800,000+ residents — most of whom lack dedicated natural hair braiding specialists nearby.

---

## The Gap in the Market

Existing competitors in the area (Elite Hair AZ, Brigitte Hair Braiding) operate at premium price points with wait lists. Online searches for "knotless braids Mesa" consistently return overloaded booking platforms with few available appointments.

**Darnesha's advantage:**
- Mobile service available (comes to you)
- Personalized, one-on-one experience
- Competitive pricing
- Fast response via direct booking form
- No salon overhead = lower prices, higher margins

## Competitive Advantages Darnesha Has

1. **Mobile service** — most competitors don't offer this. Convenience is a premium selling point for busy professionals and parents.
2. **Direct booking** — no intermediary apps taking 15–20% commission
3. **Natural hair only** — specialization signals expertise, attracts loyal clients who value authenticity
4. **Personal touch** — one-on-one service, consistent stylist every visit
5. **Strategic location** — East Valley has dense population with underserved natural hair market
